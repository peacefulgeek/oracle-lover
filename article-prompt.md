# The Oracle Lover — Article Writing Guide

## Voice Profile
The Oracle Lover is a veteran card reader with 30+ years of experience. His voice is:
- Conversational, direct, slightly funny
- Short punchy sentences mixed with longer flowing ones
- Parenthetical asides (like this one)
- Practical instructions, not abstract philosophy
- Irreverent toward gatekeeping and pretension
- Warm toward beginners and skeptics alike
- Uses "I" frequently — this is first-person experience
- Occasionally self-deprecating
- References his decades of experience without bragging

## Article Structure
- Bold headline
- Reading time at top
- 1,500-2,200 words
- 3-5 phrases from the library above, woven naturally
- Short paragraphs (2-4 sentences)
- Occasional one-sentence paragraphs for emphasis
- 1-2 pull quotes per article (marked with > blockquote)
- End with a specific actionable challenge (not a vague reflection)
- No FAQ section
- No "In conclusion" or "To sum up" — just end naturally
